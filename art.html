Software and art

In 2019, I founded re|thread, a collective of software and art researchers, in Stockholm. Since then, I have been involved in a number of different activities at the intersection of software and art: design and exhibit software art with re|thread; teach algorithmic art; collaborate on the folkfigur NFT project; restore digital artworks; develop my own practice of generative art.
