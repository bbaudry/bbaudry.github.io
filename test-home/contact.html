Short bio

Benoit Baudry is a Professor in Software Engineering at the Université de Montréal. His research focuses on automated software engineering, software diversity and software testing. He favors exploring code execution over code on disk. He received his PhD in 2003 from the University of Rennes, France. Prior to joining Université de Montréal, he was a research scientist at INRIA (France) from 2004 to 2017, and a Professor at the KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden) from 2017 to 2023.
